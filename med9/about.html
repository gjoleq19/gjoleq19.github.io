About us

• The business Kimay Madish was created in 2006 from the cooperation of two companies(Madish SHPK and Kimay SRL) in the field of production of military uniforms and security troops. The variety of items produced by this company came to be enriched even more, by the presence of many new customers in the field of fashion and sports.Referring to this correct and successful cooperation between the two companies, it has become possible for most of the guards and security service employees in the Italian market to order their goods from this company.

• The idea of creating this cooperation arose as a need to diversify daily production as a safer way to advance this business as well as to bring it closer to contemporary levels and with an open European market. A very good weapon for achieving the required levels (in addition to maximum dedication and accuracy) was also the positive image of our main customers Cotonela SRL and Kimay SRL based in Italy.
